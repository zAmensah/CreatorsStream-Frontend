HOW IT WORKS

Time is Money and creatorsstream.com bring this to life by enabling users to make the best of their time spent on the internet. All users basically earn money for every second of their time spent on creatorsstream.com Users are paid from advertising revenues generated by the website and every second spent on our site is worth money to the user. Users earn money from subscriptions to their channels and subscriptions they make to other channels. Essentially the more time you spend on creatorsstream.com the more you earn money for your time. Users are encouraged to create and upload videos to their channels and create traffic to their channels to increase views which will ultimately result in increased advertising revenues of your channel.
